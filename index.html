Problem

There is only about 5-10% of the undocumented immigrant population enrolling in higher education each year. From that small percentage, just 1-3% graduate. These miniscule percentages seem alarming, specially for undocumented students, who face tougher challenges in today's society.

How can we help increase both the amount of undocumented immigrants that enter college and graduate successfully?

Users & Needs

Undocumented students face many struggles that affect their hopes of becoming college students and succeeding in college. Throughout that pursue of higher education, undocumented students have many needs that include:
Guidance

Legal advice

Academic advising and tutoring

Scholarships and tuition

Career opportunities

Community

Building connections with reliable organizations

Meeting new friends

Safe spaces

Solution

Addressing the information needs of undocumented students by connecting them to a platform that encapsulates local organizations that do provide professional guidance and safe spaces.
